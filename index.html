About Us

Coddens Consulting provides Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Customer Engagement consulting. The founder, coming from a background in big-4 consulting, founded this firm with the following principles:

  • Deliver quality solutions driven by client vision and our experience
  • Lower costs by limiting unnecessary customizations and meetings
  • Empower teams by recognizing efforts and supporting work-life balance

When choosing Coddens Consulting, you can expect a team that you will want to work with. A team that will ask the questions necessary to understand your business needs, while also having the know-how and experience to deliver a best-in-class solution.

A consulting firm, but not standard consultants

It can be common for eyes to roll when bringing consultants into your meeting room, but we're here to become a part of your team for however long we're needed.

  • 01 What is the Coddens Consulting approach?

    Listen first, solution later. We want every voice to be heard when designing a solution for your business. We strive for no-code/low-code solutions, but are open to custom work when it becomes necessary.

  • 02 How does Coddens Consulting limit project costs?

    Our goal is to make Fortune 500-level consulting available to all businesses and nonprofits. While our pricing is competitive, our overhead allows us the flexibility to provide lower estimates to your business.

  • 03 What can I expect in regards to project planning?

    We consider communication to be key. Expect project timelines and effort estimates before each project phase. We rely on a SCRUM methodology to ensure that projects are progressing according to plan, and we aren't afraid to communicate risks when they are incurred.
